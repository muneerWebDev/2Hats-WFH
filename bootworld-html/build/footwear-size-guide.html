Want To Know How Your New
Boots Or Shoes Will Fit?

Want to know how your shoes will fit? You’re in luck! Boot World has developed a custom sizing scale to help guide shoe selection and fit for every piece of footwear we sell.

The Boot World Sizing Scale includes two separate scores of 1-5, one for shoe length and one for shoe width, based on the size of an average shoe. A score of 1 is extremely small and a score of 5 is extremely large. Please refer to the Size and Fit section on each product page for product-specific sizing details. Please note that all information provided here is general and is meant only as an informational guide. All manufacturers and brands have different sizing in many of their shoes.
